An unseen blend of classic and modern creations

The Horse and Groom is a traditional pub set over two floors in the popular area of Shoreditch. During the day it is the perfect place to enjoy meeting with friends or colleagues in a relaxed setting. There is also a beer garden to enjoy. At night the bar showcases various genres of music hosted by some of the finest DJs. An upstairs room is available for private hire, ideal for birthdays or work events. The exciting food menu offers succulent steaks, burgers and ribs. Drinks are plentiful including craft beers and cocktails.
